Shakshuka

Description

Shakshuka is a North African and Middle Eastern dish of eggs poached in a rich tomato sauce with spices. It's warm, comforting, and often served for breakfast, though it works just as well for lunch or dinner. The combination of spices and runny yolks makes it hearty yet simple.

It's traditionally eaten with bread for dipping, and the sauce can be adjusted with chili for heat or herbs for freshness. It's a one-pan dish, making it easy to prepare and clean up.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Eggs
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parsley

  2. Add tomatoes & spices, simmer into sauce.

  4. Cover, cook until whites set.
  5. Garnish with parsley, serve with bread.
