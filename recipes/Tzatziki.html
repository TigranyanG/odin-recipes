Tzatziki

Description

Tzatziki is a refreshing Greek yogurt-based dip made with cucumber, garlic, lemon, and dill. It's creamy, tangy, and cooling â€” perfect alongside grilled meats, in wraps, or simply served with warm pita.

The yogurt provides richness while the cucumber and herbs bring freshness. It's quick to make, requires no cooking, and improves in flavor if chilled before serving.

Ingredients

  • Greek yogurt
  • Cucumber
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Dill
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Pita bread

Steps

  1. Grate cucumber, squeeze out water.
  2. Mix with yogurt, garlic, lemon, dill, oil, salt, pepper.
  3. Chill for 30 min.
  4. Serve with warm pita.
